Frequently Asked Question

What is a surprise box?

A surprise box is a curated package filled with a variety of mystery items that follow a specific theme. The contents of the box are a surprise until you open it!

How does the subscription work?

You can subscribe to receive a surprise box on a monthly, quarterly, or yearly basis. Each subscription period, you'll receive a new box filled with exciting items.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times depend on your location. Typically, orders within the same country take 3-7 business days, while international orders may take 10-20 business days.

What kind of items can I expect in a surprise box?

Each box is themed and may include a mix of items such as collectibles, gadgets, fashion accessories, beauty products, snacks, and more. The specific contents are a surprise!

Are the items in the surprise box brand new?

Yes, all items included in our surprise boxes are brand new and sourced from reputable suppliers.
